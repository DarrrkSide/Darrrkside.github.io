Adding your own team takes under a minute

Open js/teams-data.js in any text editor. Copy the template block at the top of the file, paste it into the TEAMS list, fill in your Cards, and save. The page you're looking at right now reads directly from that file — no other code needs to change, and any new tags you use will show up as filter chips automatically.
